Mission and Vision Events and Initiatives

Built from the ground up in the Cordilleras.

AWS Student Builder Groups are student-led groups for learners focused on learning Amazon Web Services Cloud Technologies. Supported by the AWS Academic Advocacy, various Philippine Chapters offer workshops and mentorship to help students build using cloud computing.


VISION: To become a leading student-driven community that fosters globally competitive, innovative, and industry-ready individuals through mastery of cloud computing and emerging technologies.


MISSION: The guild is dedicated to providing accessible cloud computing education while also equipping members with practical skills through workshops, hands-on projects and collaborative initiatives. It fosters a culture of continuous learning and knowledge sharing within a supportive community while exposing its members to current tools, practices and career opportunities.

Through these efforts, the group aims to empower individuals to develop impactful and innovative solutions that contribute to technological and digital advancement.
